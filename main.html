Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious cuisine. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural experiences.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach - Famous for water sports and nightlife.
  2. Calangute Beach - Known as the "Queen of Beaches".
  3. Fort Aguada - A historic Portuguese fort with scenic views.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  5. Dudhsagar Falls - A spectacular four-tiered waterfall.

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Chicken Xacuti
  • Bebinca (traditional Goan dessert)
  • Vindaloo
